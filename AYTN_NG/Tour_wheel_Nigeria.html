PLACES to Tour

Obudu Cattle Ranch. It’ll blow your mind.

Visit Obudu Mountain Resort also known as Obudu Cattle Ranch in Cross river, Nigeria, for a perfectly luxurious and peaceful stay, have a few adventures like; cable car ride...located on the Obudu Plateau, close to the Cameroon border in the northeastern part of Cross River, Nigeria. It is about 30 minutes drive from Obudu town and about 332 kilometres drive from Calabar, the capital. Due to its 1,600 metres (5,200 ft) elevation, the resort experiences a semi-temperature mountain climate. The air is clean and the view is breathtakingly beautiful! This beauty was developed in 1951 by a Scot, M. McCaughley

Ogba-Ukwu Cave and Waterfall. See for yourself.

Ogba Ukwu cave is said to be the largest cave in West Africa. It’s a naturally carved rock formation in the form of an arena, large enough to take in an entire community. ⁣ The rock formation forms a natural beach around the Ogba waterfall.

Checkmate, La Campagne Tropicana.

La Campagne Tropicana Beach Resort, is entwined in the core freshness of nature. The view of the gentle reflection of the setting sun on the Atlantic Ocean is breathtaking and a must experience...

And lastly, this one. Gurara Waterfall.

"Gurara waterfalls, is an amazing place for tourism, located in Gurara, Niger State. A must visit. Beautiful atmosphere and great environment. The water is at its fullest during raining season but if you want to see more of the water and get closer, this is the best time to visit... don’t forget to wear some comfortable shoes and clothes. Stay hydrated and take some food/snacks along."
