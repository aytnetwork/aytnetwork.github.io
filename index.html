About

African Youths in Tourism Network (AYTN)

About Us:

AYTN stands as an energetic Pan-African tourism network fueled by the enthusiasm and commitment of African youth. We act as a catalyst for fostering cultural unity across Africa through our Pan-African tourism management services, simultaneously empowering the continent's vibrant youth.

Our Vision:

Our vision is resolute and ambitious: to achieve economic sustainability for Africa by 2030 through our dedicated efforts as a Pan-African tourism service non-governmental organization (NGO).

Our Mission:

Embedded in the core of AYTN is a mission deeply aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 17: "Partnerships for the Goals." We are committed to networking and empowering African youths in the realms of Tourism, Arts, and Culture.

Our Motto:

"Unity, Diversity, and Tourism" encapsulate our ethos and aspirations. We believe that unity and diversity are the foundations of Africa's rich cultural tapestry, with tourism being the key to unlocking its potential.

Our Slogan:

"Awakening you to the wonders of Tourism!" invites you on a transformative journey, discovering the wonders of Africa through the lens of tourism.

Our Values:

Our core values serve as the compass guiding our endeavors: 1. Accountability 2. Positive Energy at Work 3. Sustainability 4. Support 5. Interdependence 6. Collaboration 7. Empowerment 8. Transparency 9. Love 10. Ubuntu

Our Services:

AYTN is your portal to a world of immersive experiences, including: - Pan-African destination tours - Community-based Tourism - Sustainability programs - Pan-Afro cultural tourism M.I.C.E./festivals - Mentorship and Coaching - Film and Arts - Virtual tours - Agro-tourism

Get Involved:
